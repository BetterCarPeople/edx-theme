Overview

Welcome to Service Sucess Online!

This course was created to help guide you through all the moving parts of a successful service department. We know that the customer experience is incredibly important, and we want you to have the best customer service of any dealership around.

Our Interactive Phone Skills Course courses will provide you with the key elements needed to set appointments, drive retention, and increase customer satisfaction. We provide scripts and tools to allow your team to practice phone skills each and every day in less than 8 minutes.

This course is broken up by specific scripts so that you can work through them at your own pace. Each script is specific to campaigns for the lane. More importantly, though, each module gives you best practice advice and real life practices to implement immediately.

We may not be online when you are, but if you post a question, we will get back to you as soon as we can with an answer!
